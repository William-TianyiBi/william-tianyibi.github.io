Research Focus

My research centers on contact-rich robotic manipulation with an emphasis on co-design across mechanism, perception, and control. I focus on building systems that remain robust when uncertainty, compliance, and environmental constraints are unavoidable.

Visuo-Tactile Learning

Phase-aware multi-modal policy learning for manipulation tasks where tactile feedback is critical.

Mechanism-Driven Dexterity

Underactuated and adaptive hand designs that transfer structural intelligence to grasping behavior.

Human-in-the-Loop Interfaces

Multi-modal teleoperation workflows combining vision, force, and tactile sensing for fine control.
